Pocket Stylist

Pocket Stylist is a clothing organization and management mobile app that optimizes the time and effort required when choosing outfits. Users can visualize their current wardrobe and receive recommendations to help bring a sense of ease and confidence when selecting outfits.

Roles

  • UX Research/UI Designer
  • Problem/Solution Ideation

  • Data Synthesis

  • Wireframing and Prototyping

Tools

  • Miro
  • Figma
  • Formsite

Project Duration

  • Jan - Feb 2022
  • 3-week sprint
Challenge

Fashion is hard.

Fashion and style can be very impactful in daily life as people often relate their appearance, including outfitting and style, to their identity. However, selecting outfits is often a time-consuming and frustrating process. We set out to optimize the outfit selection process by offering people their entire wardrobe and personalized recommendations readily available.

Solution

Leverage personal wardrobe with AI-based recommendations.

Research

How can we simplify the wardrobe decision making process?

Competitor Analysis

None of our competitors curate using personal clothing.

Key insights from research provided an early indication that we could help people quickly create outfits that meet their expectations. We researched features that our competitors offer and analyzed our findings to discover what competitors offer regarding wardrobe management and recommendations.

None of our competitors have an AI-assisted stylist to curate outfits based on their current wardrobe.

Reflection

Final thoughts

  • It's important to create and implement features that align with data discoveries (i.e. guerilla testing) to make sure we're continuously catering to our users.
  • Coordination and planning among colleagues is key to successful time management and overall product delivery.

Next Steps
